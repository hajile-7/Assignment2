About us 新正陽

Indulge in a culinary adventure at Restoran Xin Zheng Yang Tea House, a bustling restaurant with a myriad of stalls offering tantalizing Malaysian delicacies. From fragrant porridge to savory pig organ soup, savor the diverse flavors that tell the vibrant story of Malaysian cuisine. Experience a gastronomic paradise where every bite is a delightful journey.

Nasi Lemak Top Seller

Discover the irresistible allure of Nasi Lemak, Malaysia's top-selling dish! Indulge in the aromatic blend of coconut rice, spicy sambal, crispy anchovies, and tantalizing accompaniments. Whether for breakfast, lunch, or a late-night snack, this vibrant and flavorful delight will transport your taste buds to the heart of Malaysian cuisine. Don't miss out on this must-try culinary experience!

Western Food Second Top Seller

Indulge in the flavors of Western cuisine, our second top seller. Savor the juicy grilled steaks, perfectly seasoned burgers, and exquisite pasta dishes that will transport your taste buds to new heights. Prepared with the finest ingredients and served with flair, our Western specialties promise a delectable dining experience that will leave you craving for more. Join us and savor the best of the West!

Ipoh Coffee Most sold drinks

Awaken your senses with the aromatic delight of Ipoh coffee, sourced directly from Chang Jiang Ipoh Coffee. Experience the rich and smooth flavors of this renowned Malaysian brew, crafted with passion and tradition. Our expert baristas meticulously prepare each cup, ensuring a perfect balance of robustness and smoothness. Indulge in the authentic taste of Ipoh coffee and let its warmth embrace you. Join us for a delightful coffee experience like no other.
